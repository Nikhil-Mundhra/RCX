What is RCX?

RCX is a property stock exchange platform that revolutionizes how individuals and institutions invest in real estate. By tokenizing properties and allowing fractional ownership, we make real estate investing more accessible, liquid, and efficient.

Our platform provides transparency, real-time portfolio analytics, and a community of property investors working together.

Our Mission

To democratize real estate investment by removing barriers to entry, enabling efficient capital allocation, and creating a vibrant marketplace where property opportunities can be discovered and traded.

Key Features

Portfolio Analytics

Real-time dashboards showing your portfolio valuation, returns, cap rates, and occupancy metrics. Make data-driven investment decisions.

Market Access

Browse and invest in curated real estate properties with transparent pricing and historical performance data.

Secure Trading

Trade property tokens in a regulated marketplace with enterprise-grade security and transparent transaction history.
